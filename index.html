About Stomv

Our journey of transformation and spiritual growth.

Our Mission

At Stomv, our mission is to empower individuals to realize their highest potential through the ancient sciences of yoga, meditation, and conscious living. We are committed to offering authentic spiritual practices that cultivate inner peace, balance, and profound self-transformation — helping every seeker lead a life of fulfillment and purpose.

Our Vision

We envision a world where individuals live in harmony with themselves, others, and nature — experiencing life with greater clarity, joy, and spiritual depth. Through Stomv's offerings, we aspire to awaken the limitless possibilities within every human being.

Our Journey

Stomv was born from a deep longing to make true spiritual wisdom accessible to all. Rooted in timeless traditions yet responsive to the needs of the modern world, our journey began with a small group of seekers dedicated to authentic inner exploration. Today, Stomv stands as a sanctuary for those who wish to move beyond limitations and experience the boundless beauty of life through conscious practice.

We offer beginner-friendly classes that introduce fundamental techniques, as well as advanced sessions for those seeking deeper practice. Our instructors provide modifications and guidance to ensure everyone can participate comfortably and safely.

Consistency is more important than duration when beginning a meditation practice. We recommend starting with just 5-10 minutes daily rather than longer sessions less frequently. As your practice develops, you may naturally want to extend the duration. Many practitioners report noticing subtle benefits like improved focus and reduced stress within 2-3 weeks of daily practice.

For our retreats, we recommend bringing comfortable clothing for yoga and meditation, a personal water bottle, any necessary medications, a journal for reflection, and an open mind. We provide all yoga props, meditation cushions, bedding, and meals. A detailed packing list will be sent to you upon registration, tailored to the specific retreat location and season.

While our practices are rooted in ancient yogic and meditative traditions, they are presented in a universal context that welcomes people of all faiths or no faith. We focus on the practical aspects of these traditions—techniques that develop awareness, presence, and inner well-being—rather than requiring adherence to any particular belief system. Our community includes practitioners from diverse spiritual backgrounds.

We offer both in-person and online options for most of our programs. Our virtual sessions are designed to provide an immersive experience with live instruction and community interaction, not just pre-recorded content. This hybrid approach allows us to serve our global community while maintaining the authentic connection that is central to our teachings.
